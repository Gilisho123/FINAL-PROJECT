Our Background

Giltech Online Cyber was founded in 2019 with a simple mission — to provide reliable, affordable, and accessible digital and cyber services to the community. We noticed many people struggling with essential services like online registrations, printing, scanning, and ICT support. That inspired us to start Giltech as a trusted hub for both individuals and businesses.

Over the years, we’ve grown into a one-stop shop offering printing, programming, IT support, data analysis, and digital branding solutions. Our passion is helping clients achieve their goals through technology, innovation, and professionalism.

Today, Giltech Online Cyber stands as a trusted partner for students, professionals, and businesses who need fast, accurate, and affordable cyber services.
